Welcome to our page!

A safe, collaborative, social space open to students and enthusiasts across any and all tech disciplines.

The aim of our society is to allow student of Computer Science and any adjacent courses the opportunity to network, socialise and learn more about the industry. Members will gain a safe space to connect with like-minded individuals, and will be provided with a social and collaborative environment for students often perceived as solitary. There are opportunities to attend events to network, socialise, get study support, and develop relevant skills.

Follow us on instagram to keep up to date with our latest events and opportunities!
